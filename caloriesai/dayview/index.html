DayView

Your meals and daily log. Take a picture—AI estimates calories and nutrition and tells you about your food.

How it works

DayView · Header & date

Step 1

Your meals & daily log

Pick a date, see your name, and export your day as a shareable image (vertical story, text only, or 2×2 collage).

Daily calories progress

Step 2

Daily calories at a glance

Total calories vs goal with a progress bar, plus protein (P), carbs (C), and fat (F) for the day.

Meals list

Step 3

Meals list

Each meal shows a photo, meal type, time, calories (with under/over/neutral color), nutrition summary, and a short story snippet. Tap for full detail.

Take or choose photo

Step 4

Add a meal with a photo

Simply take a picture or choose one from your library. AI will estimate calories and tell you about your food. Or add a meal manually without a photo.

Meal type & portion

Step 5

Meal type and options

Pick breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack. Optionally set cooking style and portion size to improve the estimate.

Optional note

Step 6

Add a note (optional)

Size, cooking style, ingredients—anything that helps estimate calories and nutrition. Then tap “Analyze meal.”

AI result

Step 7

AI result

See estimated calories, nutrition (protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar), “About this meal” story with citation, recommended reads or podcasts, and detected food items. Save to your log.

Export day

Step 8

Share your day

Export today as an image: vertical story (header + each meal with photo), text only, or 2×2 collage. Share to Instagram or anywhere you like.
