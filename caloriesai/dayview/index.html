CaloriesAI

Your meals and daily log. Take a picture—AI estimates calories and nutrition and tells you about your food.

How it works

Step 1

Your meals & daily log

Date picker, daily calorie total vs goal with progress bar, and macros (P/C/F). Your meals list with photos, times, and calories.

Step 2

Add a meal with a photo

Take a picture or choose one from your library. AI calculates calories and tells you about your food. Or add a meal manually.

Step 3

Meal detail

Tap any meal to see the full picture, calories, note, nutrition (P/C/F, fiber, sugar), detected ingredients, and a short story about the dish.

Step 4

AI result for every meal

Each logged meal gets an estimate, nutrition breakdown, detected items, and a story—from tacos to panna cotta.

Step 5

Choose an export layout

Story (text only), Story with photos, 2×2 Collage, or Strip Collage—then share your day to Instagram or anywhere.

Step 6

Share your day

Your exported summary: date, total calories, and each meal with photo and time. Track meals with CaloriesAI.
